## 🔗 **STEP 1: FIND THE DOWNLOAD LINK**

### What You're Looking For:
A file called: **OPEN-LEE-3.1.0 Setup.exe**

### Where to Find It:

#### Option A: On GitHub (Recommended)
1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, etc.)
2. Go to: `https://github.com/daddy-gier/open-lee/releases`
3. You'll see a list. Look for the latest release (top of the list)
4. Under it, you'll see file names. Find: **OPEN-LEE-3.1.0 Setup.exe**
5. Click on it
6. Click the **download arrow** button
7. ✅ File is downloading!

#### Option B: From Google Drive
1. Ask the person who gave you the link
2. Click the link they sent you
3. Click the **download** button (down arrow)

## ⚙️ **STEP 3: INSTALL THE APP**

### This is Easy — Just Follow the Wizard!

1. **Right-click** on **OPEN-LEE-3.1.0 Setup.exe**
2. Select **"Run as administrator"** (important!)
   - Windows might ask: "Do you want to allow this app to make changes?" 
   - Click **"Yes"**
3. An **Installation Wizard** appears
4. You'll see buttons and text. Here's what to do:

#### Installation Screen 1: Welcome
- Click **"Install"** or **"Next"**

#### Installation Screen 2: Choose Location
- (Usually you can just click "Next")
- This is choosing where the app goes on your computer
- Default is fine

#### Installation Screen 3: Installing...
- It's downloading and installing
- A progress bar shows how far it is
- **Wait** for it to finish (usually 1-2 minutes)

#### Installation Screen 4: Complete!
- You see: "Installation complete"
- Click **"Finish"**

### ✅ **Installation is Done!**

## 🆘 **TROUBLESHOOTING FOR BEGINNERS**

### ❌ Problem: "Windows says the app is dangerous"

**Why it happens:** Windows doesn't recognize new apps.

**Fix:**
1. Click **"More info"**
2. Click **"Run anyway"**
3. That's it!

---

### ❌ Problem: "The app won't start"

**Why it happens:** Could be several things.

**Fix (Try these in order):**
1. Restart your computer
2. Download the installer again
3. Try right-clicking → "Run as administrator"
4. Make sure you have 4GB RAM free

---

### ❌ Problem: "One of the AI models didn't respond"

**Why it happens:** The model's server is busy or offline.

**Fix:**
1. That's normal sometimes!
2. Just try again
3. Or try a different query
4. The other models will still work

---

### ❌ Problem: "Where is my downloaded file?"

**Fix:**
1. Open File Explorer
2. Click **Downloads** on the left
3. Look for OPEN-LEE-3.1.0 Setup.exe
4. If not there: Check your Desktop or Documents

---

### ❌ Problem: "I don't remember my password"

**Fix:**
1. Click the **Windows Start button**
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Click **Accounts**
4. Click **"Sign in options"**
5. Click **"Reset your password"**
6. Follow the steps

---

### ❌ Problem: "The installation is taking forever"

**Fix:**
1. Be patient! It can take 2-5 minutes
2. Don't close the installer
3. Make sure you have internet (it downloads files)
4. If it's been more than 10 minutes, close and try again
