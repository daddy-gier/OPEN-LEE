# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inspiring, and harassment-free community for all, regardless of:

- Age
- Body size
- Disability
- Ethnicity
- Gender identity and expression
- Level of experience
- Nationality
- Personal appearance
- Race
- Religion
- Sexual identity and orientation
- Socioeconomic status

---

## Our Standards

### Examples of Behavior That Contribute to Creating a Positive Environment

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started
- Providing constructive feedback
- Acknowledging and celebrating contributions

### Examples of Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment of any kind (jokes, slurs, personal attacks)
- Discriminatory language or actions
- Sexual harassment or advances
- Publishing private information without consent
- Violent threats or language directed against another person
- Trolling or flame wars
- Unwelcome sexual attention
- Disruption of discussions or events
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying expectations of acceptable behavior and taking appropriate corrective action in response to instances of unacceptable behavior.

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly.

**How to report:**

1. **Email**: [Create issue with "Code of Conduct" tag]
2. **Direct Message**: Contact maintainers privately
3. **GitHub Issues**: Create a private issue if available

### Response to Reports

- All complaints will be treated seriously and confidentially
- We commit to responding within 48 hours
- Investigation will be fair and impartial
- Privacy of reporters will be protected

---

## Consequences of Unacceptable Behavior

Depending on the severity and frequency:

1. **Warning**: First-time minor violations result in a warning
2. **Temporary Ban**: Repeated violations may result in temporary bans
3. **Permanent Ban**: Severe or repeated violations result in permanent removal

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All community spaces (GitHub, Discussions, Issues, PRs)
- Official project spaces
- Public spaces where someone is representing the project
- Interactions related to the OPEN-LEE project

---

## Our Commitment

We commit to:

- Ensuring a safe and welcoming environment for all
- Treating violations fairly and transparently
- Respecting the privacy of those reporting issues
- Taking action proportional to the offense
- Providing pathways for rehabilitation
